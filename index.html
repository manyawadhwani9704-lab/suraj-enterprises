Peanuts

We source the best Kharif-season peanuts directly from cultivator networks, offering bold, fresh, and naturally flavourful groundnuts — available raw (shelled & unshelled) and blanched, as per wholesale buyer requirements.

🥜
Raw Peanuts — Unshelled

Fresh groundnuts in their natural shell — ideal for large-scale processing, oil extraction, and further trade.

🫘
Raw Peanuts — Shelled
